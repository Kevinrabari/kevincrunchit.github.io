Slogan: Crunch your bite

Application Screen Recordings

Business is one of the main career aspects for us as Gujarati.Thus, as we are currently living in Barrie, Ontario we found a problem, especially for Indians who are students here. The variety of snacks is vast, and we Indians love it. However, due to less availability, the cost of snacks is higher therefore as students we cannot afford it. Moreover, there is less competition in this market and as we know that it is good for customers to have competition. To solve this problem, we developing an application from which students can order snacks directly to their homes. We will buy snacks directly from wholesalers and sell them on the appplication. So, it will be cheaper and time-saving for students to buy Indian snacks.

My Role

We are developing a project in a group of 5 people. So, we have decided to divide project work equally. Till now I have worked on some of the UI/UX designs and some Java files.
